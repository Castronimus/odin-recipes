Completo

The completo is basically a Chilean hot-dog

It contain the following:

  • Hot-dog bread
  • A sausage
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
  • Mayonesse or Ketchup or both

Steps to follow:

  1. Cook the sausage to the water.
  2. Heat the bread in the oven if you want to.
  3. Cut the tomatos.
  4. Crush te avocado.
  5. Once ready, put the sausage in the bread.
  6. Put the avocado.
  7. Put the tomato.
  8. Put it Ketchup, Mayonesse or both.
  9. That's it.
