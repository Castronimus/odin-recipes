Calzones Rotos

The calzones rotos is a simple but yummy Chilean recipe

It contain the following:

  • 3 cups of sifted flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup sifted icing sugar (it's powdered sugar)
  • 2 tablespoons of margarine
  • 1 egg
  • 2 yolks
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of water maybe a little more
  • oil for frying icing sugar for dusting

Steps to follow:

  1. Combine dry ingredients, flour, powders and sugar. Then the lemon zest.
  2. Immediately add the egg and then the yolks one by one.
  3. Add the softened butter and mix the dough well. Finally add enough water to form a homogeneous mass.
  4. Roll out the dough and leave it about 1/2 cm thick. Cut rectangles 10 cm by 5 cm. Then make a vertical cut of about 3 cm on them and pass one end of the rectangle through this eyelet.
  5. Fry in hot oil, they should be golden.
  6. Drain them well and leave them on absorbent paper.
  7. Finally sprinkle with powdered sugar.
  8. That's it
